The **Reference** index gathers the durable notes: how a subsystem works, a
decision and why it was made, a design that's been built. These are meant to
outlast any single change — the thing you read to understand, not to do.

This note is an *index* (`moc: true`): a note is filed here by listing
`reference` in its `parents`. It's also the default home — a note with no other
index lands here. The graph renders it large; its card on the
[Atlas](./index.html) lists its cluster.
