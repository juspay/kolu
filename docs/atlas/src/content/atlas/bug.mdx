The **Bugs** index gathers notes that diagnose a real defect and point at its
fix. A bug note names the broken behavior, the root cause once it's understood,
and the direction of the repair — the reasoning, not a tracker ticket.

This note is an *index* (`moc: true`): a note is filed here by listing `bug` in
its `parents`. The graph renders it large; the notes wired to it are its cluster,
and its card on the [Atlas](./index.html) lists them all.
