The **Features** index gathers notes that propose a capability kolu doesn't have
yet: the design, the motivation, and enough of a plan to build it. A contributor
proposal lands here too, carrying `status: proposed` until a maintainer flips it.

This note is an *index* (`moc: true`): a note is filed here by listing `feature`
in its `parents`. The graph renders it large; the notes wired to it are its
cluster, and its card on the [Atlas](./index.html) lists them all.
