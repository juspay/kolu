### Fixed

- **Terminals no longer silently freeze.** A WebSocket that died without notice — a laptop sleeping, Wi-Fi roaming, or a network/proxy dropping an idle connection — used to leave a terminal frozen until you reloaded the page. A heartbeat now detects the dead connection and reconnects on its own, so live output resumes without a reload. <PR id={1235} />
